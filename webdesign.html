Web Design

As a freelance web designer, I leverage software like Figma and Photoshop to create visually engaging and user-centered websites. I apply UI principles such as color theory, typography, and wireframe development to enhance the overall design quality.

Conducting thorough UX research allows me to understand user needs and preferences, ensuring a tailored design approach. I prioritize accessibility in my projects, ensuring that all users can interact with my designs seamlessly. My design process incorporates empathy maps to better understand user emotions and behaviors, fostering a more intuitive experience. Collaborating closely with clients, I translate their visions into functional, aesthetically pleasing designs. Through continuous learning and adaptation to industry trends, I strive to stay ahead in the ever-evolving web design landscape.
